Kaise bitaye samay, kaise manaye Holi, Diwali ya Eid; Humaare aur khushiyo ke bich 2 gaz ki doori le aaya ye COVID.

Kota.
The classes were about to conclude and so was our journey to Kota. I thought of homecoming after the conduction of April JEE-MAIN. Just then we started hearing news of a novel virus COVID-19 infiltration into India. I ignored it and stayed focused on my exam. After few days we got news of classes getting suspended and I panicked. Booked the ticket to way back home at the earliest. There's my b'day the day I had to leave Kota but I didn't care for it at all; all I wanted was not to get stucked in Kota. But that very day, the nation went into a lockdown and I started counting the days. I was dealing with something else too and to get over with it I started watching tv and web shows imprisoned in a small cubical room. I luckily got permission to book a cab and reached home after a few weeks.

Home.
The date of exam was shifiting so I started giving less time to studies =_=. Then there was the arrival of her. She was the only good thing that happened during this pandemic. We prepared for the exam together along with our silly talks 8-) . JEE-MAIN was good but ADV went below par. So, I decided to get admmitted in an NIT and dreamt about my college life. But this COVID isn't ready yet to return back my crazy, stupid, life so easily. I would've to live my freshman college life in 15.6 inch screen only. As expected, gave my first sem with group efforts(credit to my mates). Others are having mid or second sem and I am still waiting for my sem[again online :)] to get started. At present, again she's the only good thing that is happening to me.

College.
Pending indefinitely........!

-Vivek Vaibhav
